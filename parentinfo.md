## Fees, Funding, Vouchers

We are a Government approved Childcare Provider. We take funded two and three year olds. We offer 15 hours of funding with a stretched option of up to 30 hours for qualifying parents.

We accept the Governments Tax Free Childcare Scheme if provided by your employer.

We also accept fee-paying children.

Fees can be paid weekly, monthly or half-termly (unless otherwise arranged), paid within 14 days of the invoice.

Fees are £5.75 per hour per child.

We also ask for a **voluntary contribution** of £5.00 per half term as a contribution towards healthy snacks and hygiene essentials (e.g. wipes).
