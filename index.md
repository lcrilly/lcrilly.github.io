### Established 1985 | Registered charity [1022378](https://register-of-charities.charitycommission.gov.uk/charity-search/-/charity-details/1022378/charity-overview) | Ofsted rated [**GOOD**](http://www.ofsted.gov.uk/inspection-reports/find-inspection-report/provider/CARE/221801)

Litlington Pre-school offers a safe and secure setting for children to increase their curiosity and explore their surroundings, enhancing their development and confidence as they grow up in the "Big Wide World".

> _We strive to give encouragement, teach tolerance, show fairness and enjoy friendships._

We are a non-profit, community pre-school, with links to all local schools. We typically feed into

* [Bassingbourn Community Primary School](https://www.bassingbourn.cambs.sch.uk/)
* [Royston Tannery Drift First School](https://www.tannerydrift.herts.sch.uk/)
* [Steeple Morden Church of England Primary School](https://www.steeplemorden.cambs.sch.uk/)
