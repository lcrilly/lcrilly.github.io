We are a Charity run Preschool and we work alongside a voluntary committee who support us in all that we do.

We regularly invite the community to Coffee Mornings and Plant Sales and all of our fundraising events.

Pre-school children participate in random acts of kindness throughout the village including making and delivering Christmas cards and delivering any excess produce or flowers that were kindly donated to us which we were unable to use ourselves.

We also have two Recycling Banks on the Premises for the community to donate their unwanted clothes to earning extra Pre-school funds.
